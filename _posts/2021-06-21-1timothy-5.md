## 1 Timothy 5:1-2 Relating to older men, younger men, older women and younger women



## 1 Timothy 5:3-16 Windows in the local church

παραγγέλλω (PARAGGELO) -  prescribe these things

1. 1 Timothy 1:3 - *prescribe* to men to not teach strange doctrines
2. 1 Timothy 4:11 - to *prescribe* 'these' things to the congregation
3. 1 Timothy 5:7 - *prescribe* these things so they [widows] are above reproach
4. 1 Timothy 6:13 - I *prescribe* you [Timothy] in the presence of God
5. 1 Timothy 6:17 - *prescribe* the rich not to be conceited, to be rich in good works and generous and ready to share
