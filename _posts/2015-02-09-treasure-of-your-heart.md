## The Treasure of your heart

#### Luke 6:45

```    The good man out of the good treasure of his heart brings forth what is good; and the evil man out of the evil treasure brings forth what is evil; for his mouth speaks from that which fills his heart. - Luke 6:45```

A good man is a result of a good heart. A good heart is a result of the good it treasures.

How is a person good unless he/she treasures that which is good? What is good?

And Jesus said to him, 
```    "Why do you call Me good? No one is good except God alone. - Luke 18:19```

Our hearts can treasure an abundance of things. Things that are good like family, friends, work, stability, economy, helping those in need, doing the best I can to improve this world and good ol' hard work. These are all good but if you want to have a life that truly experiences good, your treasure is going to have to be in God who is the only one who is truly good. You will be truly good if your heart's treasure is God.
When our heart's treasure becomes the good things of this world, we're choosing second best and not experiencing that which is truly good.
Why did God design it this way? I think it's because He wants to be our treasure as we are His. If we would grow in knowing the good of how much God treasures us we would experience, as a result, real good in our lives.

So, I must ask, where is your heart's treasure right now?
