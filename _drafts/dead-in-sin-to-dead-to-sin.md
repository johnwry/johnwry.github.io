# From dead IN sin to dead TO sin

A World of difference

Two little words make the difference between saved and unsaved. Between being dead and alive, between being alive and dead. 

This all seems confusing so let me try to explain what I mean. 

When we were alive in the world, being enslaved to the flesh and found guilty before God because of our sin, God saw us as dead *in* sin. We were separated spiritually from God. 



However, after trusting in Christ (and when I say trusting, I mean a moment in time when we rested in the person and finished work of Jesus Christ on our behalf) we were made to die *to* sin. God now sees us as united to Himself but separated from the flesh, the world and Satan's domain. We are Christ's possession, a gift from God the Father to His Son.
